Introduction

I have been sewing for a little over a year now. I mostly repair holes and rips in clothing I already own. When I'm in the mood for a bigger project, I alter thrifted clothing. I buy almost all of my clothes second-hand. I find it fun to take old things and make them into something new.

Did you know?

Early sewing needles were made from bone and ivory. People used thin strips of animal hide to sew larger pieces together!
